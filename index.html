SAG25: Exoplanet Reflectance Spectroscopy for the Habitable Worlds Observatory

Overview:

The NASA Exoplanet Program Analysis Group has decided to charter a Study Analysis Group (SAG) to provide input to the Habitable World Observatory on exoplanet reflectance spectroscopy. SAG25 is a community study to compare and converge on the practices of the simulation and retrieval of the exoplanet reflectance spectra, with a focus on terrestrial exoplanets relevant to HWO. Participation in the SAG is open to the community.

For more information, please feel free to contact: renyu.hu@jpl.nasa.gov
